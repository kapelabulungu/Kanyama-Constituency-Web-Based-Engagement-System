Variation of CDF Allocation
Variation of the CDF allocation across Components (Community Projects, Youth and Women Empowerment and Secondary school boarding and Skills Development Bursaries) shall not be allowed unless with express permission by the Minister.
Administrative Cost
The administrative cost related to the management, disbursement, utilisation and accountability of the CDF shall not exceed 5% of the total Fund.
CDF Motor Vehicle
The CDFC shall procure a brand new 4×4 motor vehicle from the allocation for Community Projects to be used strictly for CDF activities. The cost of the vehicle shall be amortised over a period of five (5) years. The vehicle shall be maintained from funds under the Community Projects Component.
