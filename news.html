By Mary Kachepa

Police Warn Criminals Using Fake Car Number Plates

By Joseph Katai
The Zambia Police Service has embarked on an exercise to curb criminals who are using fake vehicle number plates to commit crimes in the country. Police Deputy Public Relations Officer DANNY MWALE says the police are working with the Road Transport and Safety Agency -RTSA- among other law enforcement agencies to curb such crimes. In an interview with ZNBC News in Lusaka, Mr. MWALE said the police will not be lenient on any individual involved in such criminal offences. He has also urged car hiring firms to take precautions especially with the people they offer their services.
